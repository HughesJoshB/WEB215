William "Frollie" Hughes

Team Captain and Senior Angler. Born and raised in Ocean Isle Beach NC, he's always been on or near the water. Starting back in the 60's with surfing the break at the pier the saltwater has been in his blood for a long time. He started honing his king mackerel skills on the piers at Johnny Mercer and Crystal Pier on Wrightsville Beach. Back in the early 70's while in college and transfered that back to Ocean Isle pier when moved he moved back.

Up until about 2000, then he started fishing with "Playin Hooky" fishing team in king mackerel touranments in the Southern Kingfish Association and the old US Anglers.

Josh Hughes

Josh Hughes born and raised in Ocean Isle also, son of "Frollie" came into fishing in the late 2000's mainly fun fishing on their 19ft Pioneer. After catching a couple decent kings he asked about fishing tournaments. The first tournament that they entered was the U.S. Open and caught a 27.8lb fish that ended up tieing them for 55th place.

From there his love for the saltwater has been multipled with wanting to be on the water in touranments looking for big kings. He is our social media guru, web page developer and video editor, and jack of all trades on the boat.

Josh Benton

Born and raised in Whiteville, NC Josh Benton has a draw to the saltwater that any weekend he's not working at OVM Finicial and taking care of his little one. He can be found in his bay boat chasing drum, trout or flounder. Having played college baseball with Josh Hughes they had stayed in touch over the years and talked about fishing. After doing some near-shore fishing with his dad and family members he wanted to do more offshore fishing. With getting in contact with Josh Hughes he joined the team in 2013. With joining the team he has kept the team laughing wether on days where the bite is going off or slow days. If you get a chance ask him about his "Cobia stories" from fishing with us.

Jon Sweeney

Jon Sweeney joined the team in 2016, with working with Josh Benton at OVM Finicial they where always talking fishing wether inshore or offshore. Jon brings a wealth of knowledge from his days of working on head boats bottom fishing. When on the boat you can find him with the cast net early in the mornings looking for bait, eager to be trying to jig up bait in the area we are fishing, or helping set the spread of baits out.

Scott Milliken

Scott joined the team back in 2014. He can be found on the front of the boat jigging to working the lines in the spread on the back. Always on the look for bait when fishing and looking for bait early in the morning he's been a valuable asect on the boat when ever work alows him to have the weekend off from repairing power outages.
